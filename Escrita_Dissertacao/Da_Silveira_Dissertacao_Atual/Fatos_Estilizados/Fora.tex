Transformação de Yeo-Johnson:


\begin{align*}
\psi(\lambda, y) = 
\begin{cases}
((y+1)^\lambda - 1)/\lambda & \text{se } \lambda \neq 0, y \geq 0\\
\log(y+1) & \text{se } \lambda =0 , y \geq 0\\
-\lbrack((-y+1)^{2-\lambda}-1) \rbrack/(2-\lambda) & \text{se } \lambda \neq 2, y < 0\\
-\log(-y+1) & \text{se } \lambda = 2, y <0
\end{cases}
\end{align*}
